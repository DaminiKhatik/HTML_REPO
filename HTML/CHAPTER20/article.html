Article Element part 1

Theelement contains self-contained content like articles, blog posts, user comments or an interactive widget that could be distributed outside the context of the page, for example by RSS.

When article elements are nested, the contents of the inner article node should be related to the outer article element.

Article Element part 2

When the main content of the page (excluding headers, footers, navigation bars, etc.) is simply one group of elements. You can omit the in favour of the element.
